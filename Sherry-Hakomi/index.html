What is Hakomi?
Hakomi is a body-centered psychotherapy and mindfulness-based approach developed by Ron Kurtz in the 1970s. It integrates principles of mindfulness, somatic awareness, and experiential learning to facilitate personal growth and healing. The central idea of Hakomi is that our experiences, beliefs, and patterns are often deeply embedded in the body, and by accessing these bodily sensations, individuals can gain insight into their unconscious beliefs and behaviors.
